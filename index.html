What is the reproductive justice?

Reproductive recognizes that reproductive health is a fundamental human right and that individuals should have the freedom to make decisions about their own bodies, sexuality, and reproduction without facing discrimination, coercion, or violence. This includes access to safe and affordable contraception, comprehensive sex education, abortion care, prenatal care, childbirth options, and support for parenting and raising families. Reproductive justice also acknowledges the intersections of race, gender, class, and other social identities that impact access to and experiences with reproductive healthcare and services, and advocates for policies and practices that address these systemic inequities.

We, the undersigned, urge you to recognize the fundamental importance of reproductive rights for all individuals. Access to comprehensive reproductive healthcare, including contraception, safe and legal abortion, and prenatal care, is essential to ensure that individuals have the ability to make informed choices about their own bodies and futures. These services are crucial to the health and wellbeing of people and their families. Unfortunately, reproductive rights continue to be threatened by political and social forces that seek to limit access to these essential services. Laws that restrict access to abortion and contraception disproportionately affect those who are already marginalized, including people of color, low-income individuals, and those living in rural areas. Such restrictions often force people to resort to unsafe and illegal methods, putting their health and lives at risk. We call on policymakers and community leaders to protect and expand access to reproductive healthcare services for all individuals, regardless of their race, gender, sexuality, or socioeconomic status. We demand the right to make our own choices about our bodies and our lives, and we ask for your support in ensuring that these rights are protected and preserved. We urge you to take action now to promote and protect reproductive rights for all.
